Our Story

The Adarsh Open Learning Academy opened its doors for the first time in June 2002, in response to a need for an alternative means to educate students. It is an informal institute, serving the needs of more than 100 students per year. It allowed students to explore their own interests, more than the traditional educational environment.

The institute is dedicated to the cause of imparting education to those who couldn't get the opportunity to complete the school education on regular basis due to one reason or the other. Failed students get frustrated and become aggressive and non-trusting towards adults. There are chances that these students grow up as delinquents with many behavioral disorders.

Adarsh Open Learning Academy was developed to enable these students to get an opportunity for further education.

Our Mission

The mission of Adarsh Open Learning Academy is to improve the education of children and to inspire qualities of leadership, confidence, responsibility and intellectual risk taking among the students.

We see in education the opportunity to build a better society. We recognize the increasing interdependence of all people, the need for cooperation, negotiation, and conflict resolution in order to prepare our students to become effective participants in a changing world community.
